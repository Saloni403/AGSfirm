About ASG Advocates & Solicitors

ASG Advocates & Solicitors is a reputed full-service law firm dedicated to delivering innovative, client-focused legal solutions with integrity and professionalism. Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ's

Que. Can I legally settle my loan if I’ve defaulted?

Ans. Most banks prefer a negotiated one-time settlement over long legal battles. We negotiate and secure the best possible outcome for you, based on your situation.

Que. Is it legal to stop recovery agent calls?

Ans. Absolutely. Recovery agents must follow RBI guidelines. Threats, abuse, or public humiliation are illegal. We take legal action to protect you from any such behavior.

Que. How does call forwarding work?

Ans. We give you a dedicated number. You simply enable call forwarding on your phone for recovery calls. Our legal team takes the calls, and you’re no longer directly disturbed.

Que. Will this affect my credit score?

Ans. Settling a loan may impact your credit score temporarily—but it is better than continued defaults and harassment. We guide you on how to rebuild credit post-settlement.

Que. Can you help with a stay from DRT or court?

Ans. Yes. In critical cases, we file for Debt Recovery Tribunal (DRT) stay to stop banks from taking coercive actions like property attachment or auction.

Que. I have multiple loans. Can you still help?

Ans. Definitely. We specialize in multi-loan restructuring and settlements . Our team can create a practical legal roadmap to ease your debt burden.
